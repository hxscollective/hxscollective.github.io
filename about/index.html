About Us

H × S is a collective where two different worlds are meeting — the world of illustration and the world of code.

Our main objective is to explore alternatives ways of artistic expressions via programming, generative art and the power of web3 technologies which allow a unique interaction with artists and collectors and to be a part of the artistic process directly.
